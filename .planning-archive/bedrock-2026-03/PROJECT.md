# Mosaic R&D — Bedrock Ocean Interview Project

## What This Is

A full R&D package to demonstrate product vision for Bedrock Ocean's Mosaic platform — an ocean bathymetry data aggregation and visualisation product. The output is interview material: a competitive analysis, a product strategy with roadmap, and a working prototype/demo that can be walked through live.

Bedrock Ocean surveyed and owns proprietary bathymetry (seafloor) data. Mosaic is the commercial vehicle for that data, primarily targeting offshore wind farms and subsea cable companies. The product currently lets clients view maps and download raw data files — functional but underdeveloped relative to what these clients actually need.

## Core Value

Show Bedrock Ocean that you understand their product better than they do, and hand them a clear path forward — backed by a live demo they can feel, not just a deck they can read.
