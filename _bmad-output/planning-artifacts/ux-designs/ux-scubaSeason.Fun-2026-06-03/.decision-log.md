### D10 — Typography: Noto Sans (functional, legible, no decorative display face)
Source: globals.css --font-sans
Decision: No custom display typeface. Noto Sans for all text. Size scale from shadcn/Tailwind defaults. Research-tool aesthetic: legibility > personality.

### D11 — Product positioning in UX: "honest evidence" not "travel brochure"
Source: product-charter.md
Decision: Copy, hierarchy, and visual weight all reinforce "research tool". Hero is a strip not full-bleed. Data is primary. Affiliate CTAs are secondary and disclosed.

### D12 — Evidence confidence UI: 3-state visual language
Source: stories-v2/06, stories-v2/08, stories-v2/10
Decision: Confirmed presence (solid coral icon) / High likelihood (outlined icon) / Rare/uncertain (ghost icon + tooltip). Applied to species, sightings, reef health cards.

### D13 — No light mode, no dark toggle
Source: ux-notes.md §5
Decision: Deferred. Single dark theme for MVP.

### D14 — Reef state badge system: 3 states
Source: project_data_strategy.md memory
Decision: Thriving (teal), Under pressure (amber), Witnessing change (muted/grey). Applied as IUCN-style flat badge on cards and detail pages.

### D15 — Accessibility floor: WCAG AA
Source: PRD NFR3
Decision: All text on dark bg must meet 4.5:1 contrast. Keyboard-navigable. Globe has list-view fallback. Filter chips keyboard-removable.
