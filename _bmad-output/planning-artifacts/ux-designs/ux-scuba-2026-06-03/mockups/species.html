About this species

Oceanic manta ray

One of the ocean's largest animals and among its most reliably predictable encounters. Oceanic mantas aggregate at cleaning stations — specific sites where they hover while small fish remove parasites — making them unusual among pelagic species in that you can plan to see them. The trade driving population decline is the gill raker market, primarily in China, where dried gill plates are sold as a health product with no scientific basis.
