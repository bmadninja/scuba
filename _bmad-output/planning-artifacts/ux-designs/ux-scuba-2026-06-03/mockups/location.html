The highest recorded marine biodiversity on Earth. Walking sharks that move on their fins, manta rays at predictable cleaning stations, and coral cover that has genuinely increased over the past decade — at a time when most of the world's reefs are moving in the opposite direction. This is one of the few places on the atlas where the data is unambiguously good news.

Getting there

Nearest hub

Sorong, West Papua

Sorong Airport (SOQ) · domestic connections via Jakarta or Makassar

Transfer to sites

Speedboat from Sorong

45 – 90 min to dive sites. Most operators handle transfers from Waisai ferry terminal.

Live-aboard option

Several live-aboard routes depart Sorong and cover multiple Raja Ampat sub-regions. Best for covering Blue Magic and remote sites.
