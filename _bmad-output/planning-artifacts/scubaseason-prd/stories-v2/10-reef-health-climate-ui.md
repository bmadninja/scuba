# Story 10 — Reef Health and Climate UI On Site/Location Pages

## Story

As a diver researching a destination, I need a clear reef-health and climate section that shows what is changing, how confident the data is, and how to travel responsibly.

## Context

This is an educational and emotional product surface. It should create attention and urgency without slipping into unsupported last-chance tourism.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC1: Site/location pages show a reef-health section when data exists.
- AC2: UI separates observed condition, current thermal stress, and projection.
- AC3: Each card shows source, date, confidence, and limitations.
- AC4: Visual status is understandable at a glance.
- AC5: Responsible travel/conservation CTA appears where climate concern is shown.
- AC6: Unknown data state is explicit and does not imply safety or health.

## QA Notes

- Verify a page with no data says unknown, not healthy.
- Verify source/methodology drawer or section is visible.
- Verify projection copy avoids unsupported precision.
