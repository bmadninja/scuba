# Story 5 — Beginner and Returning-Diver Recommendation Flow

## Story

As someone new to diving or returning after time away, I need safe, beginner-appropriate recommendations for certification, refresher paths, easy destinations, and starter gear.

## Context

This flow should feel supportive and safety-conscious. It should not imply scubaSeason certifies divers or replaces instructor judgment.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC1: Beginner flow recommends learn-to-dive destinations and training paths using calm conditions, easy logistics, and course availability.
- AC2: Returning flow recommends refresher-friendly destinations and operators where available.
- AC3: Recommendations include safety limitations and source links to training/safety guidance.
- AC4: Starter gear recommendations are separated into "rent first," "buy early," and "buy later."
- AC5: Beginner recommendations avoid advanced-current, deep, liveaboard-only, or tech sites unless clearly marked as future inspiration.
- AC6: All recommendation explanations are editable without changing ranking code.

## Source Requirements

- PADI / SSI style course references where used
- DAN safety/refresher guidance
- Operator/course availability where claims are operator-specific
- Site condition data for difficulty and season

## QA Notes

- Verify no beginner flow recommends advanced-only sites as immediate next steps.
- Verify safety caveats are visible.
- Verify gear funnel does not overpush expensive equipment too early.
